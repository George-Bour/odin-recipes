Pasta

Image from epicurious.com, recipe mine :)

Description

Pasta is a food that not only is very easy, it can be made with so many ways that makes for a really good food for the busy, or the ones that want to make something delicious in a pinch!

This recipe will be my own rendition of pasta with tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta No.6
  • Tomato sauce (I use one already prepared, you can use fresh tomatoes for the ultimate taste).
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil
  • (Optional)Grated cheese

Steps

  • For the pasta

    1. Fill a pot half with water and let the water boil
    2. Throw the pasta in the pot
    3. Throw some salt in the pot, not a specific amount, it depends on your preference
    4. Stir the pasta and continue stirring at random intervals so the pasta doesn't get stuck to the bottom of the pot
    5. Get the pot out of the heat
    6. Throw some olive oil and stir

  • For the tomato sauce

    1. Pour some olive oil into a small pot and let it boil a little bit
    2. Pour the tomato sauce into the pot
    3. Put two to three spritz of salt
    4. Put two to three spritz of pepper
    5. (Optional) I like to put two spritz of oregano because i like the taste
    6. Let it boil until the sauce is starting to lose i'ts liquid form (unles you want it to be watery like)

And at last serve by pouring the now ready sauce on the pasta! Also throw some of the grated cheese on it depending on your tastes!
