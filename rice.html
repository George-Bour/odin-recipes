Basmati rice

Image from hungrylankan.com, recipe mine :)

Description

This recipe will be Basmati rice. I'll make the plain rice iteration. You can serve it with lots of different ways depending on your tastes!

Ingredients

  • A cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • 3 table spoons of olive oil

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice with water. That way it will not be gooey when it gets cooked.
  2. Put the cup of rice in a pot.
  3. Add 2 cups of water in the pot.
  4. Add 3 table spoons of olive oil
  5. Sprinkle with pepper.
  6. Sprinkle with salt.
  7. Put the pot to the stove to start boiling.
  8. Once it starts boiling, turn down the heat and let it boil for 15 minutes.
  9. Turn off the heat, take the pot out of the heat and put a towel on top of it closing it's lid.
  10. Leave it like that for 3 minutes. We do this so the towel can absorb all the water of the rice, that will come out with steam.
  11. It's ready!
